## Complete synthetic text

On an island, a small lighthouse discovers that its beam cannot reach boats behind the black cliffs. One windy night it pulls its stone feet from the ground and walks to the other side of the island. The boats turn safely toward the harbor, but the lighthouse's old garden is left in darkness. The lighthouse must decide whether to stay, return, or invent another way to share its light. The story ends before the decision is made.
